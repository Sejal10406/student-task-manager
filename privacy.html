Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Study Task Tracker is an open-source application designed to help users manage their daily study tasks. We respect your privacy and ensure your data remains secure.

2. Data Collection

This application does not collect or store any personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details.

3. Local Data Storage

All tasks are stored locally in your browser. This means your data stays on your device and is not sent to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

This application does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertisements.

5. User Control

You can edit or delete your tasks anytime. Clearing your browser storage will remove all saved data.

This policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
